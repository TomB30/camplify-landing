Plan, schedule, and coordinate all camp events and activities in one centralized platform. Whether you organize by cabin base or camper choice, our auto-scheduler does the work for you. Effortlessly assign cabins, track capacity, and manage accommodations for campers and staff. Our intelligent automation handles room assignments, roommate preferences, and special needs — so you can focus on creating the perfect camp experience.
