FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Camplify. Still have questions? Drop us a message.

How does the auto-scheduling feature work?

Camplify's auto-scheduler analyzes your programs, staff availability, camper preferences, and facility constraints to automatically generate conflict-free schedules. You choose the scheduling mode — cabin-based or camper-choice — and our engine handles the rest in seconds.

Can Camplify handle both day camps and overnight camps?

Yes. Camplify is built to support both day camp and overnight camp operations. Whether you need housing assignments, meal planning, and overnight supervision tools, or just daily activity scheduling and attendance, the platform adapts to your camp's structure.

Can I manage multiple programs and sessions simultaneously?

Absolutely. Camplify supports multiple concurrent sessions, age groups, and programs within a single account. You can switch between sessions easily and view a customized dashboard that gives you a real-time overview of all active operations.

How are camper and staff records kept secure?

Security is a top priority. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We follow enterprise-grade security standards, enforce role-based access controls, and never sell or share your data with third parties. Your campers' information stays private.

Does Camplify support calendar export and reporting?

Yes. You can export your full camp calendar to Excel or PDF. Camplify also includes customizable dashboards with real-time metrics on attendance, participation, meal counts, and more — so directors always have the data they need.

How do I get started with Camplify?

Getting started is easy. Request a demo using the form below and our team will set up a personalized walkthrough tailored to your camp's size and needs. Onboarding is guided and straightforward — most camps are fully set up within a day.
